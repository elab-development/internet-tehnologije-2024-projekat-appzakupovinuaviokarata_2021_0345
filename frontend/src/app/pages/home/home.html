Finding flight deals: frequently asked questions

How can I find the best flight deals?

Search for flights, but don’t enter a departure date. Choose Whole month, then select Cheapest month. You’ll see which days are cheapest to fly out and back to Serbia.

If you’re flexible on where to go, try Everywhere search to see the best fares from your chosen airport.

Already have dates and a destination? Set a Price Alert to get notified when fares rise or fall.

Sign up to get the best weekly flight deals straight in your inbox.

How can I find the best last minute flight deals?

Look for off-peak departure times (very early or late), mid-week flights, and nearby airports. Filter by shortest or cheapest and compare across multiple providers before booking.

How can I stay updated on flight deals and low fares?

Create Price Alerts for routes you care about and monitor the fare trend graph. Consider flexible dates and let the calendar highlight cheaper days.

What happens after I’ve booked my flight?

You’ll receive a confirmation from the provider. Double-check passenger details, baggage rules, and visa requirements. Add your booking reference to the airline’s site/app to manage seats and check-in.

Where should I book a flight to right now?

Use Everywhere to discover the best-value destinations from your airport this month. Sort by price and filter by trip length, stops, or emissions to match your plans.
